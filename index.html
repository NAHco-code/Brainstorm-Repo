Developer to know: K. Wilkinson
UX - UI - Full Stack

Kathryn Wilkinson has experience working in a variety of fields, as well as an established background in the arts.


"My background in the service industry and as a social worker has taught me how to effectively work with people on a wide array of levels; whether it be conflict resolution, training and education, creative problem solving, marketing, sales, or working with a team."

She grew up in Columbus, Ohio with three brothers and their parents, who continue to teach math & music in the Hilliard City School District. She now lives with her partner and their black lab, Stark.

INSPIRATION

Revive Connection

K. Wilkinson is a visionary - combining vivid imagination with a desire for practical solutions. This conceptual creative is the ideal collaborator, as she is fueled by the spark of potential she sees in everything and everyone.

Kathryn is passionate about elevating the human environment surrounding sustainability, mindfulness, and connection to life & the planet that supports it.

New Arcadian Heights Company is an idea that aims to define the direction and ultimate destination for humanity and it's relation to all forms of life, as well as Earth itself.

Education

K. Wilkinson has earned a Bachelor of Science from Miami Univeristy of Ohio, and a Full-Stack Web Development Certificate in JavaScript from Ohio State University through Trilogy Education Services.
